Earthquake news

Bangladesh is getting frequent earthquakes

Bangladesh sits in a seismically active zone, making earthquakes a real risk for the country. Although major quakes are not frequent, the nation is vulnerable because of its location near active fault lines and the dense population in cities like Dhaka. Many buildings are not built to strong earthquake standards, which increases the danger if a large quake occurs. Authorities and experts regularly warn that improved preparedness and stronger construction are important to reduce damage and keep people safe in the future.
